FLIPTURN

BIO:

Flipturn was formed in 2015, when a group of Florida classmates, with a shared love for music and explosive chemistry decided to see what happened...
The chemistry during their first practice was undeniable. They had to share that chemistry and energy with fans and that soon began to happen.

Flipturn's music has since spread both nationally and internationally. The band has over 12 million streams on Spotify and there is no stopping them.
Their latest album "Something You needed" was released in February 2020 and as of January 2021 they are already writing the next release.
